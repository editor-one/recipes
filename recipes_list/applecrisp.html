Apple Crisp

This apple crisp showcases apples at their best! The combination of warm,
tender apples and the crunchy oat topping is one of my favorite desserts. Delicious with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 4.5 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Place the apples in a baking dish.
  2. Sprinkle the apples with white sugar, flour, and cinnamon.
  3. Drizzle water over the apples.
  4. Make the oat topping, then sprinkle it over the apples.
  5. Bake until the topping is golden brown and the apple mixture is bubbly.
