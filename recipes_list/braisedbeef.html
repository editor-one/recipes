Braised Beef

What Is Braised Beef?

Braising is a cooking method that involves slowly cooking meat in a covered pot with some sort of liquid.
In the case of this recipe, chuck roast is braised in a mixture of beef broth and red wine. Braising is similar to stewing,
but it requires less liquid.

  • 1.5.2 kg beef (chuck or brisket)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup red wine or beef broth
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • Thyme and bay leaf
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce or Worcestershire sauce (optional)
